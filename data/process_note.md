# Lead Intake Process Note

## Intended Workflow Rules

This workflow is designed to move new leads from intake to booked meeting quickly and consistently.

### Expected operating rules
- All new leads should receive a first response within **4 business hours**.
- A qualified lead should have a documented next step before the end of the same business day.
- Any lead with **no touch for 48 hours** should be escalated to the team lead or pipeline owner.
- High-value leads should never remain untouched for more than **1 business day**.
- Lead ownership should stay clear from intake through scheduling to reduce handoff confusion.

### Process intent
The purpose of this workflow is not just to create activity. It is to make sure qualified demand gets timely attention, moves forward consistently, and does not stall due to preventable follow-up failures.

### What good performance looks like
- timely first response
- low stalled lead rate
- clear ownership
- visible next steps
- strong conversion from new lead to booked meeting

If the team sees rising delays, missed follow-up, or a growing number of untouched qualified leads, those should be treated as early warning signs that the process is drifting.
